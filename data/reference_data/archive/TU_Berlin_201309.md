## 💼 Area of Responsibility (Tasks)
- [ ] Project work in the BMBF project MALSCO: "Machine Learning for SANS from Colloids".
Task is the investigation of structural changes during colloidal reactions by analysis of scattering data with methods from Machine Learning with the aim of a substantially improved product control. ln this context an existing reactor set-up shall become improved further and be used for the study of different colloid and polymer reactions in which reaction parameters become systematically varied. Central aim is the analyis of the obtained scattering data (primarily small-angle neutron scattering) by methods from Machine Learning for an optimised interpretation of these data. This work will be done at the Institut Laue-Langevin, a world-leading international research institute in Grenoble, France, und at TU Berlin, where the primary place of work can be flexibly decided.
