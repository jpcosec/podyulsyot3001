## 💼 Area of Responsibility (Tasks)
- [ ] The research tasks include the Investigation of spintronic Terahertz emission processes after excitation with ultra-short light pulses. In particular dependencies on the wavelength, from the meV to KeV regime with a focus on the UV to X-ray regime, the pulse energy, the pulse duration and pulse form shall be investigated. An additional focus is on the development of a robust electro-optic detection scheme that allows to detect THz waveforms, pulse resolved and sensitive. Moreover, contributions will be made to the development of a X-ray pulse property monitor in collaboration with the European XFEL GmbH.
