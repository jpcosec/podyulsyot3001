# PhD Application Master Template

This document contains reusable components for applying to PhD and Research Assistant (Wissenschaftlicher Mitarbeiter) positions, specifically tailored for German universities like TU Berlin.

---

## 1. The Degree Equivalency Paragraph (Crucial)

*Use this in your cover letter (motivation letter) or in the initial email outreach to professors to preemptively clear up any confusion about your 5+ year Chilean degree vs. a standard Bachelor's. Since you have the Anabin certificate, you are fully qualified.*
